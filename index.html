Mukono, Uganda

Official Flag of Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa and boasts one of the richest natural heritages in the world, with a variety of ecosystems. It boasts over 1,000 species of plants and animals, many of which are found nowhere else in the world. It is famous for its mountain gorillas, located in the Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, and some of the most beautiful landscapes in the world, such as Lake Victoria, the source of the Nile River, and the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains. Equally interesting is Uganda, with its diverse culture comprising more than 50 ethnic groups that have contributed to its dynamic traditions and history.
